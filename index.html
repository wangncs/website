Short Biography (CV)

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Ning Wang. I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer and Information Sciences, Temple Univeristy, Philadelphia, PA, USA. My advisor is Prof. Jie Wu.

I obtained my Bachelor's Degree from School of Physical Electronics, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China.

I am interested in modeling, routing, and scheduling related problems in Opportunistic Mobile Networks, Mobile Edge Computing (one of key techniques in 5G and Smart Cities), and Location-aware Services.
